Interactive Quiz

You will now do an interative quiz.

As in the previous quiz, you will see displays with four spaceships each, with one of them highlighted. Your tutor sees the same spaceships, without the highlighting, and in a different arrangement.

Your task is to describe the highlighted spaceship. Your tutor will try to figure out which spaceship you were referring to. You will score a point if your tutor guessed the right ship ‒ so try to be precise!

You can only use words from the previous quiz. That is, you can use `the', `spaceship', colors, and words from the Alien language. Your tutor won't understand other words.

If you're unsure, just try ‒ you can edit your response and resubmit any number of times. Alternatively, you can just press "Skip" to move on.

If your score drops too low, the tutors will go through some material again. The HIT will finish earlier if you're doing well.

Advanced Quiz

You're doing a great job. You will now do a more advanced quiz.

You will see displays with four spaceships each, with one of them highlighted. Your tutor sees the same spaceships, without the highlighting.

Your task is to describe the highlighted spaceship, by clicking on the words on the right. Your tutor will try to figure out which spaceship you were referring to. You will score a point if your tutor guessed the right ship ‒ so try to be precise!

Use "Erase" to erase the description.

It is possible to describe the spaceships using only the words provided. If you're unsure, just try ‒ you can edit your response and resubmit any number of times. Alternatively, you can just press "Skip" to move on.

If your score drops too low, the tutors will go through some material again. The HIT will finish earlier if you're doing well.
